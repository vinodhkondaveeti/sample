Introduction

Welcome final year students! This webpage is designed to provide you with essential resources and information to help you succeed in your final year of education. Whether you are preparing for exams, looking for project ideas, or planning your next steps after graduation, you'll find valuable resources here.

Resources

Here are some useful resources to assist you in your studies:

  • Khan Academy - Free educational videos and exercises.
  • Coursera - Online courses from top universities.
  • Google Scholar - Search scholarly articles and papers.
  • edX - Online courses from leading institutions worldwide.

Timeline

Here is a suggested timeline for your final year:

  1. August - October: Focus on core subjects and review previous coursework.
  2. November - December: Start preparing for final exams, work on project proposals.
  3. January - March: Intensive study for final exams, finalize projects.
  4. April - June: Final exams, submit projects, graduation preparation.
